Netjr House — Scientifically Planned Affordable Housing in Fayoum

Overview Value Business Model Buy a Unit Founders

Project Overview

Netjr House is a scientifically planned housing project developed on a 134 m² plot, designed as a safe and durable replacement for informal settlements in Al-Sa‘ayda Al-Qibliya Village, Fayoum Governorate.

Value Propositions

  • Scientifically planned housing units
  • Affordable housing for low- and middle-income families
  • Climate-responsive design suitable for Fayoum
  • Improved infrastructure and living standards

Business Model

Customer Segments

  • Low-income families
  • Middle-income families
  • Residents of informal settlements
  • Age group: 20–60 years

Key Partners

  • Ministry of Housing
  • Fayoum Governorate
  • Cement suppliers
  • Steel suppliers
  • Local contractors

Key Activities

  • Architectural design
  • Structural calculations
  • Construction and finishing
  • Quality inspection

Key Resources

  • Portland cement
  • Reinforced steel (12–16 mm)
  • Red bricks
  • Engineering team
  • Local workforce

Cost Structure

  • Cement: 90,000 EGP
  • Steel: 110,000 EGP
  • Bricks: 40,000 EGP
  • Labor: 120,000 EGP
  • Infrastructure: 60,000 EGP

Total Estimated Cost: ≈ 420,000 EGP

Revenue Streams

  • Sale of housing units at subsidized prices
  • Long-term installment payments (20–25 years)
  • Government housing funding
  • Public–Private Partnership contributions
