Project Overview

Netjr House is a scientifically planned residential housing project designed as a durable alternative to informal settlements in Fayoum Governorate.

Why Netjr House

  • Scientifically planned housing units
  • Affordable long-term ownership
  • Climate-responsive design
  • Improved infrastructure

Target Buyers

  • Low- and middle-income households
  • Residents of informal settlements
  • Age range: 20–60 years
