Project Overview

Netjr House is a scientifically planned residential housing project designed as a safe and durable alternative to informal settlements. The project targets low- and middle-income families in Fayoum Governorate through a transparent, government-aligned ownership model.

Why Netjr House

  • Scientifically planned housing units
  • Affordable long-term ownership
  • Durable construction materials
  • Design adapted to arid climatic conditions
  • Improved infrastructure and living standards

Target Buyers

  • Low-income households
  • Middle-income households
  • Residents of informal settlements
  • Age range: 20–60 years
  • Residents of Fayoum Governorate
